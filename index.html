CS390VR - Spring 2017

CS390VR is a course designed to teach you how to create virtual reality experiences using modern game development packages. Students will have a choice of Unity or Unreal Engine for each project, and have access to the HTC Vive, Oculus Rift DK2, and Leap Motion devices for their projects.
