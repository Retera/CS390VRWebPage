Lighting

In Unity, a light source can be rotated or positioned just like other GameObjects. Essentially, it is a gameobject that is attached with a light component.

Challenge:

Look for the properties of this component and see if you can change its color or range!

Materials and Textures

Materials are an important part of the experience you design. They define how the surface of a shape will be rendered.

We suggest this tutorial for learning materials.

You might also want to check out the Official Unity Tutorial about Materials. It is shown below.

Camera

This section will briefly go over information about Cameras, which define how your user will look into the world. Cameras will make it easier to transform actors to perfection.

First, make sure to have one and only one Camera object in your scene hierarchy.

In Unity, you can adjust the position and rotation of the camera the same way you adjust the transformation of other objects in your scene.

To create your Camera, right click on the hierarchy panel and select “Camera” if you don’t have the “Main Camera” on there.
